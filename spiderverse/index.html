WHAT IS THE SPIDER-VERSE?

Essentially, the Spider-Verse serves as a broad term encompassing the various iterations of Spider-Men and Spider-Women from alternate universes within the Marvel universe. Over recent years, these diverse versions of Spider-Man have encountered one another, joined forces, and faced challenges together, often depicted in The Amazing Spider-Man comic series.
